What Is VoteCaster?

VoteCaster is a site that allows you to quickly make a poll that you can share with your friends. Anyone can view the list of polls and the results, but only users can create or vote in polls. To make your experience as fluid as possible, VoteCaster was built on the MEAN stack, ensuring optimal user experience.


Works Globally

The majority of web traffic today is on mobile devices, so to make sharing your poll as easy as possible, VoteCaster will work on all your devices! Built on a flexible framework, Votecaster was build to run on screens of all shapes and sizes.
